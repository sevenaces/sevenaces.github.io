Information Vizualization - for the digital age
Cause processes are important (insert smug face)
  • #1 Get the data

    D'uh.

  • #2 Understand the data

    More important than you think. Most of your visualization happens here.

  • #3 Visualize the data, on paper

    Build your hypothesis. Think about the various degrees of abstraction you would want your visualization in. What do you want to do with it? Or if you want to play with it. How would you?

  • #4 Clean the data

    The most boring state. You will never get data which is ready for visualization. Based on the concepts you want to reproduce, sort your data out.

  • #5 Build

    This is where most action will take place. Build paper prototypes if you need to. Anything. The goal is to communicate.
