Four years ago,we saw Ayush Mahesh Khedekar, 12,as little Jamal Malik in the Oscar-winning film Slumdog Millionaire.Now,the childactor will be seen as Varun Bapat,a student,in Gandhi of the Month, which also stars Hollywood actor Harvey Keitel.Khedekar, who studies at Ramaben Babubhai Kanakia School in Mumbai,will also play the lead in Ek Tha Hero, which is scheduled to release later this year.It's about a boy's quest to own a cycle.Yet another heartwarming story.

STAR WARS
Pooja Salvi, 25,and Evelyn Sharma, 23,complete the happy picture in director Rohan Sippy's untitled romantic comedy,with Ayushmann Khurrana and Kunal Roy Kapoor.Sharma is half-German and has two films lined up--Issaq with Prateik and Ayan Mukherji's Yeh Jawani Hai Deewani.Salvi,on the other hand,will make her film debut. The Mumbai model is a regular on television,having done ads for Cadbury,Tata Sky and jewellery brand Francis Alukkas.The question is,who will audiences prefer?

SHERAWAT WITH BANDERAS

Mallika Sherawat's habit of getting herself photographed with foreign celebrities has got Hollywood actor Antonio Banderas into trouble at home. He may have been grinning as the two danced at US billionaire Paul Allen's party at Cannes in May, but his wife Melanie Griffith isn't as thrilled. Looking at the videos we aren't surprised, either. So what's next, Mallika?
