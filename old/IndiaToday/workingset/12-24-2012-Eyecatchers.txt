E Y E C AT C H E R S

Kahaani director Sujoy Ghosh is used to calling the shots but he will be taking orders from Rituparno Ghosh for his debut film where he plays the popular fictional Bengali detective Byomkesh Bakshi. "I was shocked. I thought he wanted me to direct behind the scenes," he says.

Smooth As Silk
It's the season for super secretive celeb weddings. Giving tough competition to Kareena Kapoor and Saif Ali Khan are Vidya Balan, 34, and Siddharth Roy Kapur, 38, CEO of UTV Motion Pictures. That the two will eventually wed was long known, but not a peep was heard from either till their first pre-wedding Mumbai event on December 11 that leads to a wedding three days later. We wish them luck.
