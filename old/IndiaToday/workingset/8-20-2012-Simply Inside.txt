OUR PICK of the month
Korn'cert India
September 5 Two-time Grammy award winner, American metal band KORN is set to perform in India. The band will be performing in Gurgaon, Mumbai and Bengaluru on 5th, 7th and 9th September. Known for songs like Blind and Thoughtless, the band will be performing for the first time in India. However, the band is already connected to Indian fans through Facebook. The band members who will be performing in India include Jonathan Davis, James 'Munky' Shaffer, Reginald 'Fieldy' Arvizu, and

Ray Luzier. So Delhiites, get set for some head banging music this month. Tickets Available at bookmyshow.com; Rs 1,450 to Rs 16,000 At Gurgaon (Venue not yet finalised) Tel 012439895050
