Transport Bloodlines of India

A visualization showing the major roads and railways (intentionally without labels) using TileMill and MapBox, as a part of a GeoVizualization Workshop by Arun Ganesh.

One of the primary attempts was to use the metaphor of bloodlines (veins and arteries) to show the transport network in one of the most populated nations in the world.

It is an Interactive Map and can be zoomed in and out as well as one can pan around. Zooming in shows even the finer roads.
