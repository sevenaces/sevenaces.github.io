Semiotic Analysis

The following is a study of the public structures in Bangalore, Karnataka namely the following :

  1. Sri Basava at Besveshwara
  2. Sri Rajiv Gandhi, Seshadaripuram
  3. Swami Vivekananda, Malleswaram

The study deals with a semiotic study of the statues, and tries to understand the meaning behind these structures on various aspects.
