Sound of Vision

As a part of the Design Project II, Information and Interface Design at National Insitute of Design, R&D Campus Bangalore

The brief for the 6 week project was to create a digital device to make lives of a pre-selected user type.

For this project, I had selected my target group as the Visually Challenged.

The final device was a wearable strap-based digital device which with the help of a head-mounted camera enables the wearer to sense his surroundings through music as well as textural feedback. This helps blind users to navigate, recognize objects and faces from far, as well as get an emotion connect to their surroundings.
