Transport Schematic Map of Bangalore

A schematic map of Bangalore showing major roads, railways and even the newly started metro track.

One interesting self-goal for this was to keep all roads at an angle of 45°, which I stuck to, except for the MG/Cubbon Road.

This was made by overlaying a map of Bangalore obtained from the GIS Data, on Adobe Illustrator.
