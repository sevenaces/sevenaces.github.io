State of States
How healthy are the United States of America

An interactive information visualization done to visualize a big database, i.e. The Health Records of the United States of America. I had data from multiple sources, like the IRS, US Census, the World Data Bank and the Center of Medicare and Medicaid Services. These databases were collated to create three indexes Disease Prevelance Index, Insurance/Tax Index & Hospital Coverage.

The concept was to use a heartbeat as a graph coupled with a chloropleth chart. The peaks of the heartbeat were used for the three indexes.
The same can also be used to show health of a system

The cognitive breakdown of understanding goes as follows

  • First Glance: compares system with normal set standard
  • Second Glance: focuses down to problem areas
  • Third Glance: tells us what exactly is wrong.

The visualization uses HTML/CSS for Front-End, a JSON Database, and uses D3.js for all the processing and visualization of data.

More details on the process and other information can be found on Behance.
